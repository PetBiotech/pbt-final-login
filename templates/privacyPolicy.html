Privacy Policy

Our website is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains the types of personal information we collect and how it is used and shared.

Collection of Personal Information

We collect personal information from users who submit samples for diagnosis through our website. This information may include name, contact information, medical information, and sample information.

Use of Personal Information

The personal information we collect is used to process and analyze the submitted samples for diagnosis. It may also be used to contact users for follow-up or to provide results. We may share your contact details and address with our sample pickup partner, Bluedart for the purpose of collecting the samples.

Sharing of Personal Information

We may share your contact details and address with our sample pickup partner, Bluedart for the purpose of collecting the samples. Any third parties that receive personal information from us are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and are only allowed to use the information for the purpose for which it was shared.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the personal information we collect from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date above and post the new policy on our website.
